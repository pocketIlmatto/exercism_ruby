# Food Chain

Write a program that outputs the lyrics of the song 'I Know an Old Lady Who Swallowed a Fly'

This is a [cumulative song](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cumulative_song) of unknown origin.

This is one of many common variants.

```plain
Verse 1
A I know an old lady who swallowed a fly.
B I don't know why she swallowed the fly. Perhaps she'll die.

Verse 2
A I know an old lady who swallowed a spider.
C It wriggled and jiggled and tickled inside her.
D She swallowed the spider to catch the fly.
B I don't know why she swallowed the fly. Perhaps she'll die.

Verse 3
A I know an old lady who swallowed a bird.
C How absurd to swallow a bird!
D She swallowed the bird to catch the spider that wriggled and jiggled and tickled inside her.
D She swallowed the spider to catch the fly.
B I don't know why she swallowed the fly. Perhaps she'll die.

Verse 4
A I know an old lady who swallowed a cat.
C Imagine that, to swallow a cat!
D She swallowed the cat to catch the bird.
D She swallowed the bird to catch the spider that wriggled and jiggled and tickled inside her.
D She swallowed the spider to catch the fly.
B I don't know why she swallowed the fly. Perhaps she'll die.

Verse 5
A I know an old lady who swallowed a dog.
C What a hog, to swallow a dog!
D She swallowed the dog to catch the cat.
D She swallowed the cat to catch the bird.
D She swallowed the bird to catch the spider that wriggled and jiggled and tickled inside her.
D She swallowed the spider to catch the fly.
B I don't know why she swallowed the fly. Perhaps she'll die.

Verse 6
A I know an old lady who swallowed a goat.
C Just opened her throat and swallowed a goat!
D She swallowed the goat to catch the dog.
D She swallowed the dog to catch the cat.
D She swallowed the cat to catch the bird.
D She swallowed the bird to catch the spider that wriggled and jiggled and tickled inside her.
D She swallowed the spider to catch the fly.
B I don't know why she swallowed the fly. Perhaps she'll die.

Verse 7
A I know an old lady who swallowed a cow.
C I don't know how she swallowed a cow!
D She swallowed the cow to catch the goat.
D She swallowed the goat to catch the dog.
D She swallowed the dog to catch the cat.
D She swallowed the cat to catch the bird.
D She swallowed the bird to catch the spider that wriggled and jiggled and tickled inside her.
D She swallowed the spider to catch the fly.
B I don't know why she swallowed the fly. Perhaps she'll die.

Verse 8
A I know an old lady who swallowed a horse.
E She's dead, of course!
```
